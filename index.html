Book of the Week

The book club is excited to announce that the book of the week is Gerogre R.R. Martin’s fantasy epic A Game of Thrones. A new York times best seller and that has been turned into the must see TV series of the century.

Game of thrones is set an a fantasy world where rival trides fight for control of all the land. Filled with massive battles and passionate love scenes its no wondering why it is a huge success.

Join the book club in reading this book and head over the discussion section to give your thoughts on it.

Discussions

Here at The Book Club, there is one thing we love just as much as sitting down to read a good book, that is talking about books. Come join other members to talk about all thing’s books. From thoughts on the book of the week, to book recommendations, discussions about authors or thoughts on up and coming titles.

We welcome anyone young or old as long as you have something to say about books. Click the button below to start your book chat today!
